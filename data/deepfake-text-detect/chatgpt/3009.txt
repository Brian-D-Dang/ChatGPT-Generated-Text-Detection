Socialism and its later potential follow up Communism, are great ideas and in an ideal world would create an utopia, where everyone is truly equal and there is no gap between rich and poor. An end to the extreme disparity of wealth has been a long-standing goal for many progressive thinkers dating back well before capitalism came into existence (for example: Plato). The problem arises when 99 of people believe they deserve more than 1, which leads me to why we have the republicans vs democrats divide on issues like this
