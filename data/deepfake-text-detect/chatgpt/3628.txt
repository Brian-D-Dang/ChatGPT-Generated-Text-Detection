In turn-based games, especially RPGs, random battles are a common feature where you'll be randomly thrown into battles with enemies as you explore the game world. The battles usually take place on a separate screen with a different interface from the regular gameplay. The frequency and difficulty of these battles can vary depending on the game and the player's progress. Some players enjoy the challenge, while others find it frustrating, but it's a staple feature of many classic RPGs.
