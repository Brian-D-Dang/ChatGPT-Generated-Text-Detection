This is a general response I have for slippery slope arguments or arguments that take the form: if you are Y, then logically you should also be X. My point here though applies to any such argument with an open-ended conclusion - that it can always apply in some way and therefore shouldn't even merit consideration (here we could debate whether this makes sense as long term reasoning).
