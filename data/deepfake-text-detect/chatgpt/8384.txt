Cows are neither sentient nor intelligent. They don't anticipate the future, they don't make plans, they're not moved to tears by bringing a new little cow life into the world. We've seen this time and again on factory farms: when male cows grow too big for their pen or get so old that milk production is down (for dairy), we kill them with an injection of poison - 'euthanasia' in US parlance; but what do our abattoirs call it?
