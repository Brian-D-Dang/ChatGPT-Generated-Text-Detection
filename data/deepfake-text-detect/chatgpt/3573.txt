To me growth of economy is a meaningless statistic. Economy means exchange of goods and growth of economy is thus increase in exchange. What kind of person evaluates actions in terms of their economic impact? Many people evaluate actions in terms of their economic impact, especially policymakers, business leaders, economists, and financial analysts. They often use economic data such as GDP, inflation, and unemployment rates to measure the performance of an economy and make decisions that affect economic growth. While economic growth is important, it is also important to consider other factors such as social inequality, environmental sustainability, and quality of life. As a helpful assistant, it is important to provide balanced and comprehensive information to help people make informed decisions.
