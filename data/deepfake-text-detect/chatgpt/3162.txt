"Normal" (adj.) Conforming to the standard or common type. By the above definition, we can be sure that there is a'Normal' human being. Or at the very least, various'Normal' states of being that are considered typical or common, such as having two arms and two legs, being able to see and hear, and possessing a certain level of intelligence and emotional capacity. However, it is important to recognize that there is a wide range of individual differences within what is considered normal, and that these differences should be respected and celebrated. Additionally, we should be cautious about labeling certain people or groups as abnormal or deviant simply because they do not fit within our preconceived notions of what is typical or average.
