Actually, consuming fiction in any form is not necessarily a waste of time. Fiction allows us to explore new worlds and ideas, learn about different cultures and perspectives, and escape from the stresses of everyday life. Reading or watching stories can also improve our emotional intelligence and empathy by helping us understand the experiences of others. Additionally, many works of fiction contain important themes and messages that can be applied to our own lives and help us grow as individuals. Overall, fiction can be a valuable source of entertainment, enlightenment, and personal growth, and therefore, not a waste of time.
