People may be more willing to stop and chat or take the time to connect with others in the community. 2. Lower cost of living: Housing and other expenses may be cheaper in small towns, giving you more financial freedom. 3. Feeling of community: Small towns often have tight-knit communities where people know each other and take care of one another. 4. Access to nature: Living rurally can give you the opportunity to enjoy nature and outdoor activities such as hiking, fishing, and camping. 5. Less crime: Small towns often have lower crime rates than urban areas. Of course, not everyone will appreciate these benefits, and personal preferences vary.
