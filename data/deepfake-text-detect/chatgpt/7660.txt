While it's true that many people may not need to use Linux for their desktop computer, that doesn't mean it isn't a valuable option. Linux offers a number of benefits that you won't find with other operating systems. For example, it's highly customizable and free to use, making it a popular choice for developers and tech enthusiasts. Additionally, Linux is generally seen as more secure than other operating systems, which is a major consideration for anyone who spends time online. Even if you don't have a specific need for Linux right now, it can be a wise choice for future-proofing your computer and ensuring that you have access to the latest technology.
