While it's understandable that Batman may not be everyone's favorite superhero, it's important to recognize the depth and complexity of the character. Batman is not just a simple character who wears a cape and fights crime, but rather a human being who has experienced great loss and trauma. His struggles with his own inner demons and his desire to do good in the world make him relatable and intriguing to many fans. Additionally, Batman's vast array of cool gadgets and impressive fighting skills makes him a formidable and exciting hero to watch in action.
