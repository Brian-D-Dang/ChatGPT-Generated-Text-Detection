As long as I've been alive, I've always poured milk before cereal. I've done this because I am a textural eater and I hate soggy cereal. I believe that pouring the milk first sets up your mouth to eat the dry cereal better by lining it with the steam from the hot liquid. I do too! But then again, I also like my breakfast so cold the cereal becomes crunchy and melts in my mouth sort of texture
