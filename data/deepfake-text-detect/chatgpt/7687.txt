While it's understandable that some teenagers may not want to devote all of their time to getting into a good college, it's important to remember that education and college can shape the foundation for one's future career and personal growth. It's critical to keep in mind that in today's society, a college degree is increasingly becoming a requirement for many jobs. Therefore, by focusing on education and securing admission to a reputable college, individuals have a greater chance of achieving success and fulfillment in their lives. Additionally, acquiring practical skills and knowledge from college experiences can help develop essential tools like analytical thinking, critical analysis, and problem-solving that can be applied in any career fields.
