Most of the people that I look up to in the world are those who are dedicated to making a positive difference in the lives of others. Her bravery and resilience are truly inspiring to me. Another person who I look up to is Dr. Jane Goodall, the renowned primatologist and conservationist. In the world of medicine, I am constantly impressed by the dedication and selflessness of doctors and nurses who work tirelessly to care for their patients. There are also countless everyday people who are making a difference in their communities, whether it's through volunteering, donating to charity, or simply being kind and compassionate to others.
