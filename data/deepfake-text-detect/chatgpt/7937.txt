While it is true that not everyone is successful at being an online entertainer, it's not entirely based on luck. The most successful online entertainers usually work hard and consistently produce quality content, build a community and engage with their audience. They also have a unique and marketable persona that sets them apart from others. Moreover, the rise of social media and the internet has created a new economy in which online influencers have become valuable assets to companies and brands. This means that being an online entertainer can offer lucrative opportunities for sponsorships, collaborations and endorsements that can bring in a significant income. So while there are risks involved, just like any career, with dedication and hard work, becoming a successful online entertainer can be a viable and fulfilling career option.
