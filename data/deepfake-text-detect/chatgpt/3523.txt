Cutthroat Kitchen is a reality TV cooking show where contestants bid on sabotages to make their competitors fail at cooking the dish that needs to be cooked, and keep the highest quality dish possible. It is hosted by Alton Brown and aired on Food Network. The show challenges contestants' culinary skills and ability to adapt to unexpected setbacks. Each round, contestants are given a sum of money to start with, and they bid on various sabotages that can hinder their opponents' cooking process. The winner of each round receives a cash prize and the chance to compete in the grand finale for a grand prize.
