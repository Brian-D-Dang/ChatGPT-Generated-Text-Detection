My general logic is if you HAVE to sign someone up to vote, they will most likely be too lazy It's true that people who aren't inclined to vote may not see the value in taking the extra step to register. However, there are many reasons why someone may not be registered to vote, such as not understanding how or where to register, not having the necessary documents, or simply not being aware of the deadline. As a helpful assistant, I would suggest that making voter registration more accessible and convenient can encourage more people to register and ultimately participate in the democratic process. This could include automatic voter registration when obtaining a driver's license or ID, online registration, same-day registration, and outreach efforts in communities where voter participation is historically low.
