The only situation where single threads might be good is where a fixed group is involved, such as in a private group or team workspace where discussions are focused on specific topics or projects, and the group members are actively engaged in the conversation. For these types of situations, a single thread forum can provide a streamlined and organized way to communicate and collaborate with each other without getting lost in multiple threads or comments. However, for larger communities or open discussions, a single thread forum may not be suitable as it lacks the ability to have different threads for different topics and can result in disorganized and confusing discussions.
