The host country thing has run its course. Organizing the Olympic games is financially ruinous, blatantly wasteful and the procedure favors corrupt regimes willing to put themselves on the map. Athletics has become so specialized that the Olympic Park has become more than a sports facility, it is a showcase of modern architecture, with an 8-acre park and its lawns and gardens covering 170 acres. The park is located on the banks of the River and has a lake which is the centerpiece of the complex.
