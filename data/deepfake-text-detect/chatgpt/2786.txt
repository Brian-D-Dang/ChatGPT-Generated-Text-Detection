The Earth is constantly changing and moving in predictable patterns and cycles. The water cycle and nutrient cycles (carbon, nitrogen, oxygen, sulfur, etc.) are Earth's metabolism. The Homo sapiens component of the Earth's biological system is also known as civilization and culture. In this section on earth science I will provide you with information about what makes up our planet through its various components including: Atmosphere, crust, mantle, core, oceans, biosphere, hydrosphere, cryosphere, meteorology, weather, theories, discussions, articles, teachers' guides, videos, experiments, and more.
