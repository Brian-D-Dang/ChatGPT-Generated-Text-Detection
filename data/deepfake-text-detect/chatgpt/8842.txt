About the Author: Angela is a doctoral candidate in political science at Star University and member of the faculty of political science at Lancaster University. She was previously a director at the Department of Earth and Planetary Sciences at Pacific Northwest State College, where she was also director of the National Center for Atmospheric Research's Global Forecasts Office. Angela studied the impact of climate change on population growth and population - and has been writing since 2011 about how this impact impacts our planet and humanity.
