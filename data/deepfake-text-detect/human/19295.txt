Causality is king. Provided you can know enough information about events, you can trace them forward or backward in time accurately, all the way down to the trajectory of individual atoms. This applies to galaxies in the cosmos, and dandelion fuzz floating on earth. This idea bothers people because its the classic assault against "free will." So lets say I "chose" to wear a red shirt this morning. My choice was just the culmination of life history resulting in an end sum. Any effort you make to break this end sum is just part of the life history leading up to your actions. you cannot escape. Philosophers who talked about predetermination were right all along.
