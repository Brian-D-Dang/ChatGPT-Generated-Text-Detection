The data was proven correct, but the weapon continues on it's trajectory. The scientists are panicking, afraid of it turning back. They furiously type messages into the machine's data analyzer. All is silent, as the readings which indicated thought had disappeared, and it's communication ceased. It kept going however, and a message appeared. "Processing" Those ten seconds of processing lasted an eternity to the poor scientists. They scrambled to call their families, to pray to various gods, and to swear at the military commanders. All the computers simultaneously pinged, and the scientists rushed to them. Four simple words flashed brightly on the advanced monitors. "Let there be light!"
