Actually, the only real treatment to specific phobias is exposure therapy. So, fear conditioning is not only possible, but also the only confirmed treatment to phobias. And the earlier you start the conditioning, the higher will be the probability of achieving the cure. No amount of drugs can help you with fear. Phobias are anxiety disorders and anxiolytics can help you cope with some crisis, but that's all they do, you won't be treated. TL;DR: Yes.
