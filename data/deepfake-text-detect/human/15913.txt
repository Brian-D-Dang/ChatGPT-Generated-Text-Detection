As of the census of 2000, there were 427,652 people, 140,079 households, and 97,915 families residing in the city. The population density was 4,097.9 people per square mile (1,582.2km2). There were 149,025 housing units at an average density of 1,427.9 square miles (3,698 km2). The racial makeup of the city was 50.2 White, 8.4 Black or African American, 1.6 Native American, 11.2 Asian (about a third of which is Hmong), 0.1 Pacific Islander, 23.4 from other races, and 5.2 from two or more races. Hispanic or Latino of any race were 39.9 of the population.
