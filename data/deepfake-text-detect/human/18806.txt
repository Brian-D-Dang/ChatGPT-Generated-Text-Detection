I believe that unless a prisoner is unable to work, they should be forced to work, not only to pay for their own accommodation in prison, but any profits should go to foundations supporting the victims of their crimes. Failing to work would result in getting fed nothing but bread and water and instead of a nice cell, a hall they share with the rest of those refusing to work. Of course, this is a reeducation facility, so I would give the prisoners who do an outstanding job certain privileges, including the possibility to get a degree in a field andor help to reintegrate you into the society. My view has been changed. While I still believe that prisoners should work, now I believe that they should be given the opportunity to work, instead of being forced. I believe that within that system, prisons should focus on rehabilitating the inmates through their labor and those who stand out given extra privileges, up to and including degrees andor opportunities for after they leave incarceration.
