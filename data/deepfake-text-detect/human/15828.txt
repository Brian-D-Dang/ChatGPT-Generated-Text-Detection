Several French paintings entered the collection as part of the 260 paintings and miniatures (not all the works were French, for example Carlo Crivelli's Virgin and Child) that formed part of the Jones bequest of 1882 and as such are displayed in the galleries of continental art , including the portrait of Francois, Duc d'Alencon by Francois Clouet, Gaspard Dughet and works by Francois Boucher including his portrait of Madame de Pompadour dated 1758, Jean Francois de Troy, Jean-Baptiste Pater and their contemporaries.
