Here's one of the best downtown restaurant to go for a breakfast! The food is good and the service excellent. And they actually serve real maple syrup. So if you want a good place to eat in the morning: Hurry up and arrived before 9 am (to avoid line up!) relax and enjoy a nice breakfast!
